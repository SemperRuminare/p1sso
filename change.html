Changing your Mattermost sign-on method

Note: There are currently references to "GitLab" in this guide and on the Mattermost interface. This will be changing shortly to "P1 SSO".


Step 1

Log in to your Mattermost account from the web app and select “Account Settings” from the Main Menu. Select “Security” and click “Edit” next to “Sign-on Method”. Select “Switch to Using GitLab SSO” option.


Step 2

Enter your Mattermost account password and click “Switch Account to Gitlab SSO”. Enter your Mattermost MFA 6-digit pin and click “Submit”.



Step 3

Once on the Platform One SSO page, type in your P1 SSO username (email) and password and select MFA Log In. On the next screen, enter your P1 SSO MFA 6-digit pin.


Step 4

Your sign-on method has been successfully changed! To sign into Mattermost, you will now select “GitLab” under “Sign in with:” which will direct you to the new Platform One login screen where you’ll enter your P1 SSO email address, password and MFA pin.


That's it!

Your account is fully transitioned to using P1 SSO. When you log into Mattermost, you'll use the "GitLab" button. If desired, you can delete your previous Mattermost MFA pin in your MFA app since it will no longer be used.
